Cities, sights and provinces

Germany consists of 16 provinces, North Rhine-Westphalia: Nordrhein-Westfalen being the province with the highest population with over 18 million people. Second comes Bavaria: Bayern with over 12 million people and the third most populated is Baden-Württemberg with around 12 million people. Germany is also the second most populated country in the world after Russia, with over 80 million inhabitants

As you can see from the map, Germany shares borders with nine European countries: Denmark in the north, Poland and the Czech Republic in the east, Switzerland (its only non-EU neighbour) and Austria in the south, France in the southwest and Belgium, Luxembourg and the Netherlands in the west.
