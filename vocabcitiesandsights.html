Cities, sights and provinces

Germany consists of 16 provinces, North Rhine-Westphalia: Nordrhein-Westfalen being the province with the highest population with over 18 million people. Second comes Bavaria: Bayern with over 12 million people and the third most populated is Baden-Württemberg with around 12 million people. Germany is also the second most populated country in Europe after Russia, with over 80 million inhabitants

On the map above you can see the names of the provinces in English as well as in German. Berlin, Hamburg and Bremen are the three city states.

Most populated cities - Top Ten

The two city states Berlin and Hamburg dominate the list with Berlin having a population of roughly 3.5 million and the latter one having around 1.8 million inhabitants. Third comes Munich with 1.5 million people and fourth and last, the city that has more than a million inhabitants is Cologne, with 1 million people. Other cities with a significant amount of people are (in order): Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Düsseldorf, Dortmund, Essen and Leipzig.
